About the Artist

Snoopy, a profound artist that started drawing at the age of just 3 dog years, has captured hearts worldwide with his whimsical and imaginative artwork. His unique perspective, born from a canine's-eye view of the world, infuses his creations with a sense of wonder and playfulness. From abstract expressionism to classic portraiture, Snoopy's versatility knows no bounds. His iconic "Peanuts" comic strip has become a timeless symbol of childhood innocence and the power of imagination.
